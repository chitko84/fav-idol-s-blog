Awards and Acheivements

  • WWE Championships: Johnson is a ten-time world champion, having held the WWE Championship eight times and the WCW/World Championship twice.
  • People’s Choice Awards: Johnson has won multiple People’s Choice Awards, including Favorite Premium Cable TV Actor for his role in "Ballers" and Favorite Movie Actor.
  • NAACP Image Awards: He received the Entertainer of the Year Award, acknowledging his contributions to entertainment and his influence as a role model.
